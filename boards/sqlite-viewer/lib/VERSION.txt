Vendored third-party libraries for the SQLite Viewer board.

Tabulator — grid renderer
  version: 6.5.1
  license: MIT (see LICENSE)
  files: tabulator.min.js, tabulator.min.css
  tabulator.css is the Persephone theme skin from the boards-assets catalog.

sqlite-vec — SQLite vector-search extension (vec0 virtual-table module)
  version: 0.1.9
  license: MIT (dual MIT OR Apache-2.0; see LICENSE)
  files: vec0.dll (official prebuilt loadable extension, windows-x86_64)
  Loaded best-effort by scripts/db-server.mjs so vec0 virtual tables
  (e.g. mneme's chunks_vec) are queryable. Windows-only by design —
  Persephone itself is Windows-only.
